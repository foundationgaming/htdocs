About

Ark Resource Calculator

The Ark Resource Calculator is a tool to help Ark: Survival Evolved players calculate the amount of resources needed to craft items selected from the catalog. There are other resource calculators out there before this, but this was created because the other calculators were not up to date or didn't have all craftable items like dyes. All of the information here was gathered from the Ark: Survival Evolved Gamepedia.

Usage

  1. Select an item from the list on the bottom left panel. It will put that item into the selected item list on the bottom right panel.
  2. Selecting that item again will add one more to the amount selected.
  3. The amount of the item can be edited manually by clicking on the amount.
  4. The selected item can also be changed by clicking on the selected item name, and changing the name.
  5. The item list can be shown in two layouts: icon-only or list. Click the toggle button on the item panel to toggle between the two layouts.
  6. The top right panel shows the total base resources needed to craft all of the selected items.
  7. Selecting a type filter from the top left panel will show items only of the selected type. Multiple filters can be selected.
  8. To show more filters, click on the carat on the filter panel.
